M01 Prove: Business Case Report

Purpose:

Help students understand that their project leads, managers, CEOs have to make financial decisions about the value and Return of Investment to spend the right amount of time/budget, funds and resources on testing.

Back Story:

You work for a software company that is developing a mobile based inventory tracking applications. The product is in its 6th release. There is not test team, most of the testing is done by the interns. So far only minor errors have been encounters with the 5 customers you support. The company just won a contract for to support over 500 customers. With the new contract, the software is expanding the number of features to support the needs of those customers.

Write a business case for testing.

  • Identify three major issues?
  • What are the risks?
  • Create a business case to inform your company why it should allocate adequate resources for testing.
    • Look up how to create business case.
    • Include the issues, risks, and how testing would mitigate or resolve the problem.
    • Develop 2 scenarios that uses your risks, and demonstrate how your mitigation or solutions might solve the problem.
    • Audience should be a manager, so there will need to be 2-3 options that the manager can choose from and the cost benefits of each.
    • Paper should be 5 pages. (Most managers don’t like to read more than 5 pages).

Rubric:

  • 10 pts: Format: Heading, Readable, Grammar, Length, Citations,
  • 20 pts: Identification of Issues/Risk
  • 30 pts: Potential Scenarios
  • 40 pts: Resolution of Issues/Risk
